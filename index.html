The idea

Why Coaster Hub

Coaster Hub started as a meticulously kept spreadsheet — every credit, every re-ride, every park visit, all with dates. This site turns that record into something you can actually explore: maps, timelines, records, and a calendar of every riding day.

It began with one count and keeps growing — everything here comes straight from real logged rides, no estimates, no rounding. More visualizations are on the way, and eventually a way for other enthusiasts to drop in their own counts and see the same stats.
